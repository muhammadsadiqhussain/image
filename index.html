Best Plants

Lavender A fragrant herb that repels pests, attracts pollinators, and has calming properties for relaxation.

Roses Classic blooms that add beauty and fragrance, symbolizing love and enhancing garden aesthetics.

Marigolds Bright, cheerful flowers that deter pests and improve soil health through companion planting.

Tomatoes Easy-to-grow vegetables that provide fresh, nutritious fruit and thrive in home gardens.

Basil A versatile herb for cooking that repels insects and enhances the flavor of nearby plants.

Sunflowers Tall, vibrant blooms that attract bees, provide seeds for birds, and add height to your garden.

Aloe Vera A low-maintenance plant with soothing properties for skin and minor burns, perfect for home remedies.

Ferns Lush greenery that thrives in shade, purifies the air, and adds texture to any garden space.
